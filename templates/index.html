About this Demo

This app uses a TF-IDF vectorizer and a trained classifier to predict whether a news piece is real or fake. Deployed on Render with Flask.

Model: Logistic Regression / Random Forest

Input: Plain text (headline or paragraph)

How to demo
  1. Paste headline or short article.
  2. Click Detect.
  3. See prediction and confidence score instantly.
  4. For mobile, open URL and “Add to Home screen”.
